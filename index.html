Full Meeting Record

A permanent, speaker-attributed transcript of every session. No disputes. No misattributions. A living audit log of every word spoken.

The context, key discussion points, and outcomes of any meeting — condensed to what leadership actually needs. Catch up on a 2-hour session in 2 minutes.

Action Ownership

Every commitment extracted with its owner and deadline. Stop losing track of who promised what, and hold people accountable with a timestamped record.
